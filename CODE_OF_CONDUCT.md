# Community Conduct

The MissionChief Map Command Toolkit project expects technical discussion to remain constructive, relevant and respectful.

## Expected conduct

- Address the problem rather than attacking the person reporting it.
- Provide reproducible information and distinguish evidence from assumptions.
- Respect privacy and never publish credentials, private repository material or personal data.
- Avoid harassment, discriminatory language, threats, spam and deliberate disruption.
- Accept maintainers' decisions on scope, security handling and release safety.

## Enforcement

Content that compromises users, exposes secrets, harasses participants or repeatedly disrupts the project may be edited, locked or removed. Accounts may be blocked from project spaces when necessary to protect users or maintainers.

Security-sensitive concerns should follow `SECURITY.md` rather than being posted publicly.
